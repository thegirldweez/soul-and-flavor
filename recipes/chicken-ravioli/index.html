Creamy Chicken Ravioli Bake

Prep Time: 30 Min

Cook Time: 60 Min

Total Time: 90 Min

Four cheese ravioli in a creamy chicken alfredo sauce with savory spinach and red peppers.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons italian seasoning
  • 3 tablespoons garlic & herb seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon Creole or Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (garlic infused)
  • 1/2 cup chopped yellow onion
  • 12oz roasted red bell peppers
  • 8oz spinach
  • 1 22oz bag diced chicken breast or 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 40oz alfredo sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Season the diced chicken with the seasoning blend and 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  2. Heat the oil in a large deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onion and saute about 5 minutes, then add the minced garlic and saute a few more minutes.
  3. Add the diced chicken to the skillet and turn heat up to high. Sear the chicken until done.
  4. Reduce the heat to medium and add the butter. Once melted, add the alfredo sauce.
  5. Add the spinach and red peppers. Mix until well blended. Taste and add seasoning if needed. Turn heat down to low and let simmer.
  6. Cook ravioli according to package directions (boil for around 5 - 10 minutes).
  7. Line baking dish with chicken mixture and then a layer of ravioli. Repeat until full.
  8. Top with basil and mozzarella cheese. Bake on 350° for 30 minutes.
  9. Serve with garlic bread and fresh salad. Enjoy!
